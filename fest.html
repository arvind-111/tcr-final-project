YOUR TEAM, OUR MISSION

SportsFest is a sporting outreach event proudly run by our team — with over 35 sports and activities. This event falls over the long weekend in September each year, and creates an amazing opportunity for youth and young adults, from all over INDIA, to come together. We encourage team members to invite youth and young adults from within their colleges, and we love it when they bring along people from their colleges too! It’s the perfect time to meet others, try new sports, create lasting memories.
